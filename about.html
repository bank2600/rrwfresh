About RRW Multimedia

We serve clients in Floyd and surrounding counties.

At RRW Inc. We strive to provideo the best services possible to our customers. These include providing the best installation, maintenance and consultation services as well as provide the end user with the best pricing and support of products.

RRW Inc. Has 35 years of combined professional experience and can offer a wide range of low voltage services. We are a local provider who will respond to your needs quickly, effectively, and professionally. The majority of our business is in surrounding five county area and predominatley with schools and libraries. We are familiar with the constraints and budgets of both, and strive to work with them. School Districts and Libraries need to provide cutting edge technology and RRW Inc. prides itself on meeting these needs at a competitive price.
